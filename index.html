How we calculate this

Flight savings use Ascend's published average discount rates by cabin class: Premium Economy (20%), Business (35%), and First (40%). Base fares are industry averages for domestic, short-haul international, and long-haul international routes.

Hotel savings are based on Ascend's preferred property network: 3-star (10%), 4-star (20%), 5-star (25%).

Time savings calculate the hours you currently spend booking travel (researching, comparing, booking, managing changes) and value that time at your stated hourly rate. Ascend's concierge handles all of this for you.

Car rental & private aviation savings reflect Ascend's negotiated fleet rates and charter partnerships.

Bonus values (disruption handling, preference memory, hotel amenities) are shown separately and not included in the headline ROI to keep the core number defensible.

Case studies are from real Ascend members with permission. Individual results vary based on travel patterns and booking lead times.
